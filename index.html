CAPTAIN AMERICA

Captain America, alias Steve Rogers, is a Marvel Comics superhero, created in 1941, known for his
symbol of patriotism and justice, enhanced by the Super Soldier Serum for superhuman abilities,
and a prominent member of the Avengers.

IRON MAN

Iron Man, alter ego of Tony Stark, is a Marvel Comics superhero recognized for his genius-level intellect, powered exoskeleton suit equipped with advanced technology, and role as a founding member of the Avengers.

THOR

Thor, the Norse God of Thunder in Marvel Comics, wields the enchanted hammer Mjolnir and possesses god-like strength, endurance, and the ability to control lightning. As an integral member of the Avengers, Thor is a powerful and noble superhero.

BLACK PANTHER

Black Panther, alias T'Challa, is a Marvel Comics superhero known for his advanced technology, superhuman strength, and role as king of the African nation of Wakanda and the Panther Clan.
